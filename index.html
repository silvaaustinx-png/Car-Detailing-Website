About Us

We’re Austin and Robert, two 13 year olds dedicated to making your vehicle look its absolute best. From foam cannon washes and deep interior cleaning to tire shine and headlight restoration, we provide top-notch service wherever you need us.

Our Services

Mobile Service

We come to you! Using a foam cannon, we power wash the exterior and shop-vac the interior. We brush down carpets, shampoo seats, and give your vehicle a fresh, clean look.

Stationary/Base Service

Bring your vehicle to us for a complete detailing experience. We use a tornado gun for deep cleaning. Plus tire shine, and headlight restoration as an additional package.

Additional Extras

Enhance your detailing experience with tire shine, headlight restoration, to keep your car looking like new.
